About Wilmington

Wilmington is located in the southeast of North Carolina. Wilmington is a river district and has beautiful beaches. Wilmington is the a great place to bring the family. Many tourist go there to enjoy the beach. Quiet Harbor is only less than 1 mile away from the beach. In Wilmington there is a historical disrtict, nightlife, and an arts & culture section. There is ove 400 film-related credits in the Area. Some famous shows and movies that were made in North Carolina were, One Tree Hill, Dawson's Creek, Iron Man 3, Scream, and The Longest Ride.
